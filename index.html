Hi, I'm Claire.

I'm a front-end web developer, designer, and creative thinker.

Last winter, I rediscovered the love of coding I had as a child and left behind my previous life as an English teacher to jump into the world of web development. I view writing code as just that -- writing. Coding is as much a creative endeavor as a technical one and I find equal joy in creating a gorgeous layout in Photoshop or Adobe XD and in building out the Javascript that makes that layout dynamic. When not coding, I love to read, practice yoga, dance, watch RuPaul's Drag Race, and thorughly annoy my two cats.

If you have any questions about my work or just want to chat, I'd love to hear from you!
